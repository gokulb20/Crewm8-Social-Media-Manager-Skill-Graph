# Influencer Outreach

Identify and reach out to micro-influencers and creators for collaboration opportunities. The goal is authentic partnerships with people whose audience overlaps with the brand's target market.

## Purpose

Paid ads are getting more expensive. Organic reach is declining. Creator partnerships are the last scalable organic channel — but only if outreach is personal, value-first, and founder-approved.

## When to Use

- Building a creator shortlist for the quarter
- Product launch needs amplification
- Existing customers include creators who could be partners
- Competitor launched a successful influencer campaign

## Inputs Required

- Brand positioning and value proposition for partners
- Creator criteria: follower range, engagement rate, niche overlap
- Offer types available (free product, affiliate, paid, commission)

## Quick Reference

| Creator Tier | Follower Range | Approach | Offer Type |
|-------------|---------------|---------|-----------|
| Nano-influencer | 500-5K | Personal DM, relationship-first | Free access, affiliate, shoutout swap |
| Micro-influencer | 5K-50K | Personalized email/DM, value-first | Paid collab, affiliate, early access |
| Mid-tier | 50K-500K | Professional outreach via email/agent | Paid sponsorship, revenue share |
| Customer-creator | Any size | Formalize existing relationship | Ambassador, affiliate, UGC partnership |

## Procedure

1. **Build the target list:**
   - Mine existing followers and engaged commenters
   - Search by niche hashtags and topics
   - Check who shares competitors' content
   - Prioritize: audience overlap > engagement quality > follower count

2. **Evaluate each candidate:**
   - Audience demographics match?
   - Engagement rate >2%?
   - Content quality brand-worthy?
   - Natural alignment with what the brand does?

3. **Build ranked shortlist** (10-20 targets) with tier, contact method, personal angle.

4. **Draft personalized outreach:**
   - Reference SPECIFIC content they created
   - Explain why partnership makes sense
   - Clear offer: what brand provides, what creator provides
   - Soft ask: "Would you be open to exploring this?"

5. **Founder approval** required before ANY outreach sent.

6. **Track pipeline:** Contacted → Replied → Negotiating → Confirmed → Content live → Performance.

## Done Criteria

The skill is complete when:
1. 10-20 targets are identified and ranked
2. Each top-priority target has a personalized outreach draft
3. The offer is clearly stated (what brand gives, what creator gives)
4. Founder has approved all outreach before sending
5. Pipeline tracking is set up

## Pitfalls

- Spraying 100 creators with the same generic DM
- Looking only at follower count (5K with 8% engagement > 100K with 0.5%)
- Making the offer about what brand wants, not what creator gets
- Pitching a creator who just did a deal with a competitor
- Following up more than once after no response

## Verification

For each outreach draft: if you were this creator, would you reply? Does it feel specific or templated? Is the founder okay with every offer?
