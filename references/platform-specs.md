# Platform Specifications

Shared reference for character limits, hashtag rules, aspect ratios, and link placement across all four platforms. Every skill in this graph should reference this file rather than repeating platform dimensions.

## Quick Reference

| Dimension | X | LinkedIn | Instagram | TikTok |
|-----------|---|---|----------|-----------|--------|
| Character limit | 280 (premium: 25,000) | 3,000 (ideal: 900–1,200) | 2,200 (ideal: 125–150) | 4,000 caption |
| Link placement | Avoid first tweet; use reply or final tweet | Anywhere after hook | Bio link only | Bio link only |
| Best format | Threads, short posts | Carousels, story posts | Visuals + captions, Stories | Video-first |
| Hashtag density | 0–1 | 3–5 | 5–10 + 2–3 branded | 3–5 |
| Image aspect ratio | 16:9 | 4:5 (carousel) | 4:5 or 1:1 | 9:16 |
| Video aspect ratio | 16:9 or 9:16 | 16:9 or 1:1 | 9:16 (Reels), 4:5 (feed) | 9:16 |
| Max video length | 2:20 (premium: 2 hours) | 10 min | 60 sec (Reels), 60 min (feed) | 10 min |
| Safe zone | Center 80% | Center 80% | Center 80% | Center 70% (UI overlays) |
| Posting frequency | 2–3x/day | 4–5x/week | 1–2x/day | 1–3x/day |
| Best posting days | Tue–Thu | Tue–Thu | Mon–Fri | All days |

## Platform Voice Characteristics

| Platform | Voice | Hook Style | Optimal Sentence Length |
|----------|-------|-----------|------------------------|
| X | Fastest. Shortest. Most direct. | First 3 words must stop scroll. | 12–18 words |
| LinkedIn | Slightly longer. More structure. Human, not corporate. | First 2 lines before "see more." Tease without clickbait. | 15–25 words |
| Instagram | Visual-first. Warmer. More personal. Emoji-tolerant. | Visual does 70% of hook work. Caption reinforces. | 10–15 words |
| TikTok | Most casual. Caption secondary to video. Friend-like. | First 1.5s of video + text overlay. | 8–12 words |

## Content Format Rules by Platform

### X
- No markdown. Line breaks for visual whitespace.
- Thread: 6–10 tweets optimal. Each tweet stands alone.
- Auto-plug: add link to first tweet after engagement threshold (20 likes OR 60 min).
- 0–1 hashtag. Never hashtag-gore.

### LinkedIn
- No raw markdown. Single-sentence paragraphs.
- Carousels (PDF slides) outperform image posts 3:1.
- First line before "see more" is the hook — about 210 characters visible.
- 3–5 hashtags at end.
- No external links in first paragraph (algorithm penalty).

### Instagram
- No clickable links in captions.
- Hashtags in first comment OR end of caption (never mid-caption).
- Stories: link sticker for external URLs.
- Carousel posts (2–10 slides) get higher reach than single-image posts.

### TikTok
- Video-first. Caption is supplementary.
- Text overlay on video increases watch time.
- Trending audio increases discovery.
- 3–5 trending hashtags.
- Hook must land in first 1.5 seconds.
